# _TROPE _
>Trope offers a dynamic, non-passive viewing experience for users. >This web experience was built with the goal of giving both >producer and audience more control in their ability to >leverage the Internet to share their knowledge. By >converting video files to a frame by frame experience that >the viewer can very easily scroll through Trope provides >a unique and more interactive way of viewing video-like >content online. ---
>Trope takes a video file and converts it into a series >of image files, frame by frame. Each frame is then placed as >full size image filling up the browser window.
>As the user >scrolls, the length of the scroll is directly matched to a >frame in the video. So, as the user scrolls down, the frames >move forward, and as the user scrolls back up, the frames >move backwards, all in direct relation to the speed of the >scroll.
>_Currently the most common interaction when viewing >media, like videos or gifs, allows little to no control. A gif >is very passive, while a video gives you the ability to play, >stop, or pause_ ---
>With Trope you must >actively scroll to move the frames forward or backward. This >interaction gives the user complete control over how they >watch the content, at what part to start, how fast or slow >they scroll through, or which parts they want to re-watch. >Giving a more experiential >control to the user, providing much more agency and thus >a stronger ability to watch the content how they want to >watch it.
