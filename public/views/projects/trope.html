### THE QUICK AND DIRTY
>Trope is a part of a collection of inventive, engaging, and uniquely purposeful web experiences >that were built to bring to life and showcase a larger research thesis, >The Manifesto for Meaning, conducted by Alex Tosti and myself. >The Manifesto for Meaning was the culmination of a year of research, >case studies and experimental builds to create a design manifesto that encourages meaningful web design. >The goal of The Manifesto for Meaning is first to critically analyse the effects of speed >and ease orientated nature of current web design. Secondly, to implement our conclusions >into a design manifesto that illustrates alternative ways of thinking about the online space >and expands on the limitations of current design. >By demonstrating the intricacies and importance of web based interaction our Manifesto for Meaning >came to life through two web based experiences, >TROPE and THE STORYTELLER that experiment with, as well as showcase, >our implementation of interaction conscious design.

# _TROPE _
>Trope offers a dynamic, non-passive viewing experience for users. >This web experience was built with the goal of giving both >producer and audience more control in their ability to >leverage the Internet to share their knowledge. By >converting video files to a frame by frame experience that >the viewer can very easily scroll through Trope provides >a unique and more interactive way of viewing video-like >content online.
