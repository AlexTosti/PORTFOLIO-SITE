# Public Transport System --- ### A Case Study
>Completed in 2012 as a real-life case study and design proposal for an Advanced Interaction Design Course in collaboration with CUBIC at Sydney University. Our team was chosen by CUBIC for our “tag system” concept as the winning proposal and were given the opportunity to present our Design At the CUBIC offices in Sydney.
_team:_

Amelia Lewis

THE BRIEF =========
>The design Brief presented by CUBIC required us to rethink the way in which public transport in Sydney Currently works. They wanted us to consider what the user experience is and what it could be. Creating a more unified and intuitive journey for all kinds of users across all different platforms of public transport.
