Wrap Beats is an interactive DIY electronic instrument, which focuses more on the user creating a piece of visual art, that is then represented melodically, rather than learning a new instrument completely.

Upon logging onto our Facebook application guests had the choice to choose a date on our time machine. Scattered around the room, the tiny screens nestled in wooden boxes of all sizes switch to that date, displaying videos, comments, news articles, music, photographs and status updates. Creating a complete blend of personal and communal history’s as the guests explore the boxes, seeing their own memories as well as the others in the room combined with general moments of note from that time.
